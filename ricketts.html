Chicken and Noodles

Source: Robin Love.

  • 8 cups of water (plus extra if needed)
  • 1 family pack of thin sliced chicken breast
  • 10 chicken bouillon cubes
  • 1 (16 oz) bag of Reames Homestyle egg noodles
  • About 2/3 of a solo cup of cold water (for cornstarch mix)
  • 3 heaping tablespoons of cornstarch
  1. Add 8 cups of water to a large pot.
  2. Add the chicken breast and 10 chicken bouillon cubes.
  3. Cook for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally. If water level drops, add an extra cup of water.
  4. Add the bag of Reames egg noodles.
  5. Cook for an additional 20 minutes.
  6. While noodles cook, fill a solo cup about 2/3 of the way with COLD water. Add 3 heaping tablespoons of cornstarch and stir well.
  7. When the timer goes off, add the cornstarch mixture a little at a time, stirring after each addition. Add more for extra thickness.
  8. Let cool - the stew will thicken more as it cools.

Chicken Poop

Source: Robin Love.

  • 12 cups of water
  • 10 chicken bouillon cubes
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut up
  • 6 potatoes, peeled and cut
  • Green beans (canned)
  • 2/3 of a red solo cup of cold water (for cornstarch mix)
  • 4 heaping tablespoons of cornstarch
  1. Add 12 cups of water to a large pot and add 10 chicken bouillon cubes.
  2. Cut 3 chicken breasts and add to the pot once boiling.
  3. Cook for 30 minutes.
  4. Peel and cut 6 potatoes and add to the pot.
  5. Boil for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add green beans and cook for an additional 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Make the cornstarch mix: fill a red solo cup 2/3 of the way with cold water, add 4 heaping tablespoons of cornstarch, and stir well.
  8. Add cornstarch mixture to the pot and stir. Add more if you want it thicker.

Veggie Beef Stew

Source: Robin Love.

  • 1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
  • Salt and a little pepper
  • 3 small cans of tomato sauce
  • 3 1/2 cups of water
  • 3 jumbo potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 3 cans of green beans, drained
  • 1 can of corn, drained
  • 1 can of sliced carrots, drained (optional)
  1. Season ground beef with salt and pepper and cook in a skillet. Drain excess fat.
  2. In a large sauce pan, add 3 small cans of tomato sauce and 3 1/2 cups of water.
  3. Once beef is cooked, add it to the sauce pan.
  4. Place sauce pan on heat.
  5. Peel and dice 3 jumbo potatoes and add to the pan.
  6. Let everything boil for 20 minutes.
  7. Add the drained green beans, corn, and carrots (if using).
  8. Add water if needed. Put a lid on to help steam the green beans.
  9. Cook for 10 more minutes.
  10. Stir and serve.

Green Bean & Sausage

Source: Robin Love.

  • 2 packs of 2-pair smoked sausage
  • 4 cans of green beans
  • Salt
  1. Drain most of the water from 2 cans of green beans; pour the rest in with the liquid.
  2. Pour the remaining 2 cans of green beans in as-is (liquid included).
  3. Cut up all the sausage and add to the pot.
  4. Add salt to taste.
  5. Cook for about 15 minutes until fully cooked, stirring occasionally.

Potato Salad

Source: Robin Love.

  • 9 eggs
  • 4 jumbo potatoes
  • Salt
  • Bread and butter pickles (about 5 or 6 stacks of 3 pickle chips)
  • 4+ dollops of Miracle Whip
  1. Boil 9 eggs.
  2. Fill a pot with 10 cups of water.
  3. Peel 4 jumbo potatoes, slice thin, and add to the pot with salt.
  4. Cook potatoes for 25 minutes.
  5. While potatoes cook, peel the eggs then put them in the fridge to cool.
  6. Drain the potato water, put the potatoes in a bowl and set aside.
  7. Cut the eggs and dice the bread and butter pickles.
  8. Add the eggs and pickles to the potato bowl.
  9. Add 4 dollops of Miracle Whip - add more if needed.
  10. Mix well and serve.
